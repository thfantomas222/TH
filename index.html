About


Tomáš Huszaník is a guitarist, composer and producer active in the Slovak music scene. In his work, he focuses on the connection of guitar expression, detailed production and work with atmosphere and dynamics.

During his career, he worked in various bands and project lines, including the group Dennica, where he participated as the main composer, producer, recording and concert activities.

His musical expression is mainly influenced by psychedelic rock, jazz fusion and modern guitar aesthetics, with an emphasis on melody, space and sound character. His main influences are Pink Floyd, Larry Carlton, Michael Landau, Robben Ford and Dežo Ursiny.
