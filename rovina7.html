Even Ground

Even Ground is the debut EP by Rovina 7, a project centered on instrumental sound, space, and movement.

Seven tracks built on minimal forms, expressive guitar textures, and dynamic flow. At its core, the EP explores the idea of balance — moving through tension and change toward a state of alignment.

Rovina 7

Rovina 7 is a studio-based musical project by Tomáš Huszaník, exploring the fragile line between structure and instinct. Rooted in psychedelic rock, jazz fusion, and immersive atmospheric sound, the project unfolds as a sonic landscape shaped by expressive guitar work, a deep sensitivity to space and emotional nuance.
